Lemon Drizzle Traybake

Extras

  • Preparation: 15 minutes
  • Cooking time: 35 to 40 minutes
  • Tin size: 30x23x4cm (12x9x1.5 inches)

Ingredients

  • 225g (8oz) butter, softened
  • 225g (8oz) caster sugar
  • 275g (10oz) self-raising flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons milk
  • finely grated zest 2 lemons
  • Crunchy top: 175g (6oz) granulated sugar
  • juice of 2 lemons

Instructions

Preheat oven to 160C / 325F / Gas Mark 3.

Grease and line tin.

Measure all ingredients into a bowl and beat for 2 minutes.

Turn mixture into prepared tin.

Level the top with a spatula.

Bake in middle of the oven for 35 to 40 minutes.

Traybake is done when it springs back when pressed or is shrinking from the edge.

Turn onto a wire rack and remove the parchment paper and place under the wire rack.

For crunchy topping, mix sugar and lemon juice in a bowl and spoon over the trayback while still warm.

When cold cut into 30 squares.
